## Test with your SVG

Download or copy the SVG files and test them directly here. You can choose icons from [Lucide](https://lucide.dev/icons) or [Tabler](https://tabler.io/icons).

<Callout>
    <>
        If you create your own SVGs, note that only `<path>` elements are supported for drawing - other SVG elements such as `<circle>`, `<line>`, or `<ellipse>` are either untested or not drawable.
    </>
</Callout>
